Mini cheat-sheet: “Is it AI?”

Usually counts as AI when…

  • It recognizes patterns (images, text, sound, behavior).
  • It makes predictions or decisions from data.
  • It improves with experience (learning) or adapts to new situations.

Not automatically AI when…

  • It’s just a regular app with if/else rules.
  • It stores data (databases aren’t “AI brains”).
  • It sounds confident (confidence ≠ correctness).
Fun fact: Some “AI” products are mostly good design + automation. That’s not bad—it just means we should describe them accurately.
